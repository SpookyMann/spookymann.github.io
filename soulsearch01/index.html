In a far away land, forgotten by time. A young girl who was desperate for answers made a deal with a demon. Through this deal she hope to find her brother who had been taken to another realm by a monster. As she signed her signature on to the contract she is suddenly pull into a portal leading to darkness.


Once she awoke, she sees that she is no longer in her world, but rather, a much more morbid one. Suddenly a heavenly magenta woman appears before her. This being claims to be her guide, a guide to her brother. The guide tells the girl that she must gather all the pieces of her brothers soul, that have been encased in demon alters. Then the guide tells that inorder to do so she must finder eyes that grant the girl powers similar to her own. Scared but desperate, the young girl ventures forth into the unknown, to save her brother, and escape this demonic realm...
